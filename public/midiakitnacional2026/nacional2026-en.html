About AWS Community Day

AWS Community Days are events organized by AWS user groups around the world, featuring workshops and networking led by industry-leading professionals. In Brazil, the event has been running since 2020 and has brought together thousands of attendees across in-person and online editions.

🎤

Our Journey

Since 2020, AWS Community Day Brasil has grown with each edition, establishing itself as the leading AWS community event in Latin America.

2020–2022 — Online editions

Virtual

Birth and consolidation of the event during the pandemic.

2023 — Return to in-person

Ribeirão Preto / SP

First in-person edition with content recorded as a podcast.

2024 — Simultaneous edition

Goiânia + Florianópolis

Expansion to two simultaneous cities, broadening regional reach.

2025 — Largest edition yet

Campinas / SP — Expo Dom Pedro

Large convention center with record attendance and high-level content.

2026 — 5 Regions of Brazil 🚀

Norte · Nordeste · Centro-Oeste · Sudeste · Sul

Historic edition covering the entire national territory for the first time.
